ABOUT IDO

WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY

IDO?

Initial DEX Offering (IDO) is a fundraising method that enables cryptocurrencies to be launched on decentralized exchanges (DEXs). This approach allows projects to raise funds through their first public token sale while giving investors early access to promising blockchain ventures.
Commonly, the funds raised through an IDO are utilized with the following principles:

For project development: Enhancing the core system, infrastructure, and ecosystem
For operations and growth: Expanding globally, increasing market adoption, and securing listings on prominent exchanges

USE CASES

Utilizations

Smart Contract

BitChangeX will leverage Stacks’ smart contract technology to power its decentralized operations. These smart contracts will enable secure and transparent execution of trades, staking, and reward distribution. Built on the robust Clarity programming language, our smart contracts will interact directly with the Bitcoin blockchain, ensuring maximum reliability and security.

Connectivity

BitChangeX will offer seamless connectivity between Bitcoin and DeFi through the Stacks ecosystem. Users will be able to connect their wallets (e.g., Hiro or Xverse) to access both Bitcoin and $BIC in a single, unified platform. Additionally, interoperability features will allow for smooth integration with other blockchains and DeFi protocols.

Earning System

Through our innovative earning system, users will be able to stake $BIC tokens to earn rewards in Bitcoin. This will be powered by the Proof of Transfer (PoX) mechanism, linking Bitcoin rewards to the staking activity of BitChangeX users. The system will be designed to provide passive income opportunities while enhancing $BIC’s value proposition.

dApplication

BitChangeX will be a fully decentralized application (dApp) that prioritizes user control and transparency. The platform will feature a user-friendly interface, accessible via desktop and mobile devices, enabling effortless trading, staking, and rewards tracking. As a dApp, it will ensure that users remain in control of their assets at all times, with no intermediaries involved.
